Tuna Mayo Rice Balls

A fantastic blend of savory and sweet mayonnaise and tuna.

Cooking Time: 15 min
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:
Rice, Tuna, Mayo, Seaweed

Ingredients Info

  • 1 bowl of rice
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Salt, seaweed

Tool

  • Hands
  • Gloves
  • Bowls

How to make

1. Prepare the Tuna
Open the tuna can and drain all the oil or water completely. Place the tuna in a small bowl, add mayonnaise, and mix well.

2. Season the Rice
Put warm rice in a bowl and mix it with a pinch of salt. Let the rice cool slightly so it's easier to shape.

3. Shape the Rice Balls
Wet your hands slightly to prevent rice from sticking. Take some rice, place tuna mayo in the center, and cover it with more rice. Gently press and shape the rice ball into a round or triangular form.

4. Wrap with Seaweed (Optional)
Cut the seaweed into small pieces and wrap or attach them to the rice ball. For a crispier texture, use roasted seaweed.

5. Ready to Serve!
Serve immediately or pack it for a quick and easy lunch.
