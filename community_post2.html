A healthy oriental medicine bossam

I have created a new version of pork suyuk, which is boiled pork. I altered the original recipe, which utilizes oriental medicinal ingredients to create delicious pork with widely available ingredients while preserving its strong herbal flavor. Your basic ingredients for making this dish include black pepper, ginger, and garlic, commonly available in most kitchens. Bay leaves will intensify the taste of the dish, yet the recipe remains delicious even without them. Create a marinade by combining soy sauce with sugar and a generous amount of black pepper and garlic before applying it to the pork's surface. Preparation time is 15 minutes. It just takes about 40 minutes to boil, but you can leave it alone for 40 minutes to get the perfect dish.
