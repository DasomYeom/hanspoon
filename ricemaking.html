Rice

Cooking Time: 30 min
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:
Rice, Water

Ingredients Info

  • 3 cup of rice
  • water

Tool

  • Hands
  • Gloves
  • Rice cooker

How to make

1. Washe three cups of rice
Wash the rice roughly four to five times with water. Third, you can use the rice-washed water as a base for soup when you boil it later.

2. Add water
Add about 4 cups of water using a measuring cup when you add rice. If you want to try the Korean method of measuring, try touching the rice bowl and adjusting the amount of water so that it comes to the line of your fingers and back of your hand

3. Waiting
Press the rice cook button, mix well with a spatula when the rice is ready and enjoy the rice
